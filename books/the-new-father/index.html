The New Father

My daughter Elichka is a year old! She's the best, let me show you a picture? Actually no. Not on the internet.

This book was a trusty reference for me when things got a bit dicey. Rather than reading daddy or mommy blogs (urgh), a quick look at the relevant age was sometimes helpful in calming the nerves. I'd recommend it to a new father.
