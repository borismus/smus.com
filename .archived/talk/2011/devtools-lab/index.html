HTML5 Development with the Chrome Dev Tools

Presented on 10 May 2011

Paul and I gave a workshop at Google I/O Bootcamp in San Francisco, CA. We walked people through real-life usage of the chrome devtools.

Slides are at http://smustalks.appspot.com/devtools-lab-11/, and the materials for this workshop are freely available on github.

I'm Boris Smus, a software engineer and UI prototyper from Vancouver, Canada. I currently live in San Francisco and work on Chrome related things.
